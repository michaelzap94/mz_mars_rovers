Methods

moveForwardByIndex(rovers, currentRoverIndex)

Move forward one grid point, and maintain the same heading. -> PROBLEM: The Rover may be at the end of the grid where move is no longer possible or will collide with another Rover Possible solution: Teletransport the Rover to the other end of the Grid, or occupy same space as the other Rover. However, this would not be a realistic solution. -> ACTUAL solution: Do not move the Rover as a move is not possible and therefore just log that a collision could happen.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
rovers number List of all Rovers in the Plateau.
currentRoverIndex number Index of this Rover in the List of all Rovers.
